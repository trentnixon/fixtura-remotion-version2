# Cricket Team of the Week — LLM design briefs (index)

Two static briefs for design LLMs (no motion or implementation):

- **Association / competition** — file `llm-brief-cricket-team-of-the-week-association.md` — **Mixed clubs** in the lineup—each pick needs **clear club / team identity** alongside the player.
- **Club account** — file `llm-brief-cricket-team-of-the-week-club.md` — **One organisation**—players may span grades/sides but **shared brand**; avoid **repeating** the same club logo on every row.

Shared: portrait artboard (e.g. 1080×1350), **grid or list of player rows** (several picks per static), optional page title and sponsor strip. Sample: `testData/samples/Cricket/Cricket_TeamOfTheWeek.json`.
